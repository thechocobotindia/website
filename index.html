Meet the maker

Hello,
I'm Gunjan.

Self-taught baker, completely eggless. Every recipe in this kitchen is one I built, tested and refined myself, over years, one batch at a time.

What I love most is showing up for someone's big day. A wedding cake. A milestone birthday. A dessert table that anchors a whole celebration. Hampers that travel. Or just a small, beautiful answer to a Tuesday craving.

Right now I'm deep in the science of dessert that does more, high-protein bakes, recipes adapted for dietary restrictions, and diabetic-friendly desserts that don't taste like compromise.
