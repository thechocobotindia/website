Going live · How to wire a real backend

This admin reads from localStorage on this browser only. For multi-device access in production:

  1. 1. Create a Google Sheet, set up a Google Apps Script to accept POSTs and append rows.
  2. 2. In js/welcome.js → saveSubscriber(), replace the local stub with fetch(SHEET_URL, ...).
  3. 3. In js/razorpay-checkout.js → saveOrder(), do the same.
  4. 4. Replace this admin page with a sheet view, a Notion database, or a simple Firebase/Supabase dashboard. The simplest: just open the Google Sheet directly on your phone.

For payment verification (HMAC signature check), you'll need a small serverless function, Vercel, Netlify, or Firebase Functions all work. ~30 lines of code, free tier covers a small bakery comfortably.
