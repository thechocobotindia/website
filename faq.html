🥚 Eggless, always

Is every product really eggless? +

Yes, every single thing. Cookies, cakes, cupcakes, brownies, loaves, hampers, all of it. This isn't a workaround. It's a decade of technique built specifically for eggless baking, proper crumb, proper rise, proper tenderness.

How do you replace eggs without it tasting "off"? +

Different binders for different jobs: yogurt, buttermilk, condensed milk, banana, aquafaba, flax. The trick isn't the substitute, it's the technique. Long cold rests, slower hydration, different fat ratios. You'll taste butter and chocolate, not a workaround.

Why eggless? +

Gunjan grew up in a vegetarian household where good dessert was hard to find. So she set out to make the dessert she would have wanted, without the egg, without the apology.

🧊 Storage & shelf life

Cookies? +

5 days in an airtight container. Pop them in the microwave for 8 seconds to revive that just-baked feel.

Brownies and loaves? +

Brownies: 5 days refrigerated. Loaves: 5 days at room temperature.

Cakes & cupcakes? +

2–3 days refrigerated. Take them out 15–20 minutes before serving so the texture relaxes.
